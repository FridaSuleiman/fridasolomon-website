About company

The largest publishing and printing center "Guzel" in Zakamye was founded at the end of 1993 by Suleimanov Adis Karievich, now the General Director, on a leased area of ​​54 m2, having only a POG-60 newspaper press and a letterpress printing area based on a PT-4 crucible press.


Today it is a diversified, dynamically developing enterprise investing in modern technology, the main direction of which is newspaper production.


The new complex of binding equipment allowed Guzel to increase productivity up to 1,000 books per day.


The successful functioning of a complex technological process became more efficient after the development and implementation of our own printing management information system GuzelStudio.
